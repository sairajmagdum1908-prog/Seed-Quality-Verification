# AgriTrust Project Information

## 🛠️ Languages & Technologies Used
- **TypeScript (.ts, .tsx)**: Used for both the React frontend and the Express backend.
- **SQL (SQLite)**: Used for the secure blockchain-simulated ledger.
- **CSS (Tailwind CSS)**: Used for modern, responsive styling.
- **HTML**: The entry point for the web application.
- **JSON**: For configuration and metadata.
- **Markdown**: For project documentation.

---

## 👥 User Roles & Responsibilities

### 🚜 **Farmer** (The End User)
- **Scan & Verify**: Instantly checks if a seed packet is genuine or fraudulent.
- **Digital Journey**: Tracks the seed's complete history from factory to farm.
- **AI AgriBot**: Receives personalized planting and care advice from the AI assistant.
- **Rewards**: Earns trust points for verifying authentic products.

### 🏭 **Manufacturer** (The Producer)
- **Batch Registration**: Adds new seed varieties and batches to the secure ledger.
- **QR Generation**: Creates unique digital identities for every physical packet.
- **Recall Management**: Can instantly flag a batch as "Recalled" to protect farmers from quality issues.
- **Analytics**: Monitors product distribution and market demand.

### ⚖️ **Admin** (The Overseer)
- **Fraud Monitoring**: Tracks "double-scans" and illegal counterfeiting patterns.
- **User Management**: Approves and manages accounts for companies and distributors.
- **System Integrity**: Ensures the entire supply chain remains transparent and secure.
- **Alert Coordination**: Works with authorities when high-risk fraud is detected.

---

## 🚀 Key Features
- **Blockchain Ledger**: Immutable record of all seed transactions.
- **Supply Chain Transparency**: Real-time tracking of seed movement.
- **AI Integration**: Gemini-powered agricultural expert.
- **Multilingual Support**: Available in English, Hindi, and Marathi.
